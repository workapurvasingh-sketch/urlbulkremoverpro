Privacy Policy for Bulk URL Removal Tool for Google Search Console

Last Updated: September 5, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Bulk URL Removal Tool for Google Search Console browser extension ("the Extension") handles your information. Our commitment to user privacy is fundamental: The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or browsing information. By installing and using this Extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The Extension is designed to operate entirely locally within your browser. We explicitly do not collect, access, or store the following:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, or any Google account details.
  • Browsing History: Your general browsing history or data from any websites other than the specific Google Search Console removals page.
  • Website Content: The content of any websites you visit.
  • User-Submitted URLs: The list of URLs you upload for processing is never sent to our servers or any third party.
  • Search Console Data: We do not access or collect any other data from your Google Search Console account.

3. How the Extension Works & Required Permissions

The Extension requires specific permissions to function, which are used strictly for their stated purpose of automating tasks on your behalf:

  • activeTab and scripting: This permission allows the Extension to inject a script ("content script") only into the specific Google Search Console Removals tab (https://search.google.com/search-console/removals). This is essential for automating the clicking of buttons and filling of forms.
  • storage: This permission is used to store your list of URLs and the processing progress locally on your device using the browser's chrome.storage.local API. This enables the pause and resume functionality and ensures your data is not lost if you close the browser popup. This data is never synchronized with our servers.
  • Host permission for https://search.google.com/search-console/removals: This permission explicitly limits the Extension's operation to the official Google Search Console Removals page. It cannot access or run on any other website.

4. Data Storage

All data generated by your use of the Extension is stored exclusively on your local device. This includes:

  • The list of URLs from your uploaded CSV file.
  • The processing progress (e.g., current index, the status of each URL).
  • The final results report.

This local data is automatically deleted if you remove the Extension from your browser.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to any third parties, including ourselves. The Extension does not communicate with any external servers. All processing is executed directly in your browser on Google's website.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the new policy in the Extension's description within the Chrome Web Store.
